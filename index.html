The Color Game

rgb(0,0,0)

Click on the square whose color is described by the RGB value above.

The three numbers in an RGB color range from 0 to 255 and describe the amount of red, green, and blue, respectively.

For example, rgb(255, 0, 0) is pure red, rgb(0, 0, 255) is pure blue, and rgb(255, 0, 255) is magenta. Pure black is rgb(0, 0, 0,) and pure white is rgb(255, 255, 255).
